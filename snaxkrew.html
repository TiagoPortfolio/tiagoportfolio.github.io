SNAXKREW is an open gaming community founded in October 2014, now going almost 3 years strong. Most of our veterans all met each other playing a small MOBA called Strife, and at our peak we held 200+ members in the game from regions all over the world, and were the largest thriving community that was always welcoming to new recruits. Since then, we have moved on to playing many different games.

Progression System

Officer
  • Event organisers
  • Must be active daily, with exceptions
  • Unlikely that this list will expand
Elite
Meet the requirements of **** and:
  • Play in several current SNAXKREW games daily (Or as best to daily as possible)
  • Introduce new games into the SNAXKREW community, becoming an ambassador and host for that game, regularly creating teams/lobbies and encouraging the grouping and playing with other members
  • Actively recruiting new members
  • If the user is not active after a period of 1 month, they will be moved to Ancient
***
Meet the requirements of Core and:
  • Play in several current SNAXKREW games at least 5 times a week, with other members
  • Active in several text channels providing content and discussion
  • If the user is not active after a period of 1 month, they will be moved to Ancient
Core
Meet the requirements of Recruit and:
  • Play in at least 1 current SNAXKREW game at least 2 times a week with other members, intergrating with the current community
  • If the user is not active after a period of 1 month, they will be moved to Lurker
Recruit
  • Active in at least text and/or voice channels for roughly one month
  • This role is based on longevity
  • If the user is not active after a period of 2 weeks, they will be moved to Lurker
Initiate
  • Active in at least text and/or voice channels for roughly one month
  • This role is based on longevity
  • If the user is not active after a period of 2 weeks, they will be moved to Lurker
